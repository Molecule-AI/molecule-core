## 3. Content Angle

Molecule AI's A2A implementation is differentiated by governance, not by the protocol itself. The LangGraph ADR validates that A2A is becoming table stakes. The brief should position Molecule AI as: "A2A works, but can you audit it?" — turning a commodity feature into a competitive proof point.

**SEO angle:** Target buyers who are evaluating enterprise AI agent platforms and comparing A2A implementations. Query pattern: "what makes one A2A implementation enterprise-ready vs. another?" Content should answer: audit trail, attribution, revocation, cross-infrastructure discovery.
